Who we are

"We're a not-for-profit organisation that sees homelessness in Dublin as a devestating social injustice"

At present this organisation is small but dedicated. We are looking to:

  1. Raise money to fund the project
  2. Build awareness of the project with the general public
  3. Establish connections with existing organisations
  4. Build a team to provide on the ground support

There is no help too little; if you would like to help in any way please contact us!

London personalised budgets for rough sleepers

Liz Blackender & Jo Prestidge on a different approach to engage rough sleepers, using a more personalised approach already adopted in social care

The personalised budgets project

Juliette Hough & Becky Rice - Fifteen people sleeping rough for between 4 & 45 years were offered a personalised budget. By evaluation, the majority were in accommodation within 13 months

Givedirectly.org - Send money directly to people in extreme poverty

Help reshape international giving. Our work is backed by GiveWell, Google.org, and – most importantly – rigorous evidence

Basic Income European Network (BIEN)

BIEN aims to serve as a link between all individuals and groups interested in basic income (i.e. a periodic cash payment unconditionally delivered to all on an individual basis)
